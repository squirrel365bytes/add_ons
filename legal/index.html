Support & Feedback BETA

Thanks for trying our Squirrel365 add-ons! These utilities are currently in beta, which means we’re actively improving features and fixing edge cases. Your feedback is invaluable.

How to Reach Us

Email: squirrel365bytes@gmail.com

Tip: When reporting an issue, please include:

  • Add-on name and version (e.g., Beeswarm v1.2)
  • What you expected vs. what happened
  • Steps to reproduce (screenshots or short screen recording help!)
  • Your environment (Squirrel365 version, browser, OS)

What to Expect

  • No personal data is collected by our add-ons.
  • No cookies, trackers, or third-party analytics are used.
  • All processing happens locally within your Squirrel365 app/dashboard.

Known Limitations (Beta)

  • Behavior may differ across browsers or when bindings are empty.
  • Designer-mode updates may occasionally require a refresh to reflect changes.
